DR. MUHAMMAD FAISAL

CEO & Co-Founder

Dr. Faisal is the Founder and CEO of Movellus Circuits. He has more than seven years of experience in the field of mixed-signal semiconductor design. Dr. Faisal is an expert in timing circuit design in advance CMOS technology nodes.
-
He received a B.S. degree in electrical engineering from the University of Waterloo, Canada, and M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from the University of Michigan, where he developed Movellus’ innovative technology under the supervision of Dr. David Wentzloff. He has authored over ten technical papers in the filed of mixed-signal design, digital signal processing and nuclear particle detection.

DR. JEFFREY FREDENBURG

VP of Engineering
& Co-Founder

Dr. Jeffrey Fredenburg is Co-Founder and VP of Engineering at Movellus Circuits. He is an accomplished analog and mixed-signal designer with prior experience in verification, testing, and characterization.
-
His past research and authorship has primarily focused on ADCs, but has contributed to the design of RF transceivers, novel oscillator designs, and highly integrated SoCs.
-
He earned his undergraduate and graduate degrees from the University of Michigan under excellent guidance of Professor Michael P. Flynn. Although his B.S.E., M.S.E., and Ph.D. are held in Electrical Engineering, Dr. Fredenburg completed extensive addition coursework in Philosophy and Nuclear Engineering while at the University of Michigan. During his graduate studies, Dr. Fredenburg was well known for leading research teams and for serving as faux advisor to many.

BOARD

SHAHIN HEDAYAT

Director
Candou Ventures

Shahin Hedayat has a passion for high tech entrepreneurship addressing complex problems through collective human ingenuity. Shahin has been an entrepreneur since 1997 starting companies with the aim of creating impactful social and economic benefits for all stakeholders.
-
Shahin co-founded 4 companies and served various roles as President, CEO, Chairman and Board Member. Those companies are Centillium Communication, Beceem Communication, Enverv and Apixio. Centillium, Beceem and Enverv reached maturation through IPO and acquisitions generating over $4B of shareholder value. Prior to 1997, Shahin held technical and managerial roles at Cirrus Logic for 11 years where he became VP of Engineering of a product line with $90M revenue.
Shahin holds a bachelor’s and a master’s degree from the University of Michigan (1981,1983) and is named on 9 U.S. patents

DR. DAVID WENTZLOFF

Director
Psikick & University of Michigan

David Wentzloff is the VP of Engineering at PsiKick and is responsible for design, tape-outs, and implementation of PsiKick’s technology. Dr. Wentzloff is an expert in low-power wireless and RF circuit design.
-
His research interests include ultra-low power RFICs for energy- and volume-constrained applications, clock generation, and IC techniques to improve synchronization of sensor networks, wireless body area networks, channel modeling, and all-digital phase-locked loops for precision frequency generation. He received a B.S.E. degree in electrical engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, and M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from MIT, where he and Dr. Calhoun first began their collaboration. Since August 2007, he has been on the faculty at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, where he is currently an Associate Professor of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science.
